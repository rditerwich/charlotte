Measure

Ultimately, what you want to understand is how the social web is affecting your business or personal brand. Some measurements are classic, but their implication is broader thanks to the social web. Campaigns and news spread faster and farther than they did before, and we can help you track whatever there's said about you. Context and discussion around your brand can advance your efforts by reflecting the language your community uses. And reach now is defined by more than just newspaper subscriptions – it's Twitter followers, influencers, commenters on blogs, or the different type of media that carry conversations about your brand.

Some metrics are evolutions. Share of voice was relevant when eyes and ears were all that mattered. Now, what you want is the right eyes and ears, and dialogue and discussion about your brand or your client's business. Whether and how you're part of the conversation.

With Charlotte you have access to a dashboard to see whether your investment in community increases your share in conversations over time, or improves your positive sentiment ratio, or adds more content-related website conversions.
